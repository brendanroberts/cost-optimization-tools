About

Margin Steward was built by Brendan Roberts, a cost optimization practitioner and software engineer based in Boulder, CO. The tools and resources here grew out of work with CFOs, controllers, and finance professionals who needed something more structured than a spreadsheet and more flexible than a consultant's slide deck.

If something here is useful, that's the point.
